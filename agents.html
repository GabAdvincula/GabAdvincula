Reyna

Reyna is a Duelist agent who thrives on eliminations,
using them to heal, become invulnerable, and enhance her combat prowess.

FUN FACTS

  • Reyna draws strength from the souls of her enemies.

  • She uses Soul Orbs from fallen enemies to heal or become invulnerable.

  • Designed for players who thrive on eliminations.

YORU

Yoru is a Duelist agent with abilities that allow him to
teleport, create decoys, and become invisible for surprise attacks.

FUN FACTS

  • Yoru's abilities are inspired by a trickster's deception and stealth.

  • His ultimate makes him invisible and invulnerable for scouting.

  • References to Japanese folklore are found in his voice lines.

FADE

Fade is an Initiator agent who uses fear and
nightmares to reveal enemy positions and control the battlefield.

FUN FACTS

  • Fade represents Turkish culture with her design and voice lines.

  • Her abilities revolve around nightmares and revealing enemy positions.

  • Unique sound cues make audio awareness crucial against her.
